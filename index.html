Citizens Advice Website

WELCOME

Citizens Advice Havering is a registered charity and member of a national network with over 18,000 volunteers and 8,650 staff, providing a vital independent confidential advice and information service to the public in approximately 2,500 locations across England and Wales, completely free of charge.

In the last year more than 2.55 million peple were given individual advice, and the website advice pages had over 58 million views.

Citizens Advice Romford

Citizens Advice Romford is the administration office for Citizens Advice Havering and is situated conveniently within the central area of Romford at Romford Central Library, which offers accessibility for all users.

Address: 1st Floor, Romford Central Library, 1 Edwards Way, Romford. RM1 3AR
